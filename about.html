About The Series

In a world where everyone seems to claim happiness is found on the outside, the Adventures of Aiden Book Series attempts to frame a different narrative: a tale of inner power that inspires children to look inside of themselves to find true empowerment, kindness, and self-love.

The creator of The Adventures of Aiden leaned into writing with the wholehearted mission to inspire each child from kindergarten to 5th grade to realize their inner power - and take that inner power with them for the rest of their lives.

Communicated through timeless lessons about bullying, self-image, following their dreams, and making the world a better place, The Adventures of Aiden series provides children with tangible tools to live with self-confidence and courage.

These tools help our readers develop positive self-perceptions, face challenges head-on, and make a positive impact on the world.

Through many winding tales weaved together by a team of characters that you can’t help but love, the Adventures of Aiden Series leads readers through journeys of challenge, fear, self-confidence and other eternal themes. And no matter what, Aiden, Skylar, and their friends always learn something valuable about themselves in each story...

And so do you!

By providing curriculums and other resources for educators, AOA’s aim is to not only motivate children, but also make it easy for teachers to incorporate the timeless lessons and themes into their lesson plans.


Whether you are a parent, educator, or other type of guardian, we hope you can use the graspable lessons taught in the Adventures of Aiden series to lead our nation’s freshest minds into self-discovery and ultimate confidence.
