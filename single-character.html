Skylar

Mary is a blue-eyed and blonde-haired master gymnast.

She’s the epitome of a sweet Georgia peach, but her favorite food is actually creamsicle ice cream. With her thick southern accent, she’s the outgoing “free spirit” of her friend group. Mary isn’t afraid of giving you her two-cents, but for such a bold young lady, she still struggles with low self-esteem some days. Sometimes this holds her back, but her full-speed approach to life balances out Aiden’s more cautious ways. Together, they make a great team that solves their hard problems together.
