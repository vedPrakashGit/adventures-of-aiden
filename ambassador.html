Ambassador of Aiden

"Building Character One Book At A Time"

Ambassadors of Aiden express mission is to facilitate the establishment of character development curriculum for youth. AOA stands ready to partner with any organization/group in order to revolutionize education that Begins Within

Who should join the Ambassadors of Aiden?

Any school, organization, civic group, or family who believes character development in our youth and our world is vitally important to us all.

What is an ambassador?

Someone who passionately represents a particular cause or movement. Here at AOA we simply believe our Ambassadors are agents for Change.

When should my school/organization consider joining the AOA?

There is no time like the present! Fill out the information on the site and we will be in touch very soon after receiving your inquiry.

Where does AOA meet and organize?

This is a great question but honestly AOA meets right where you are. Change begins within and we believe it starts with you. AOA will provide materials and support for those groups who choose to join the adventure however, the ideas and activities will be facilitated by the local group.

Why join AOA?

Ambassadors of Aiden is a non-profit which allows us to help you partner with business in your local community to provide books, curriculum, workshops and seminars to inspire the next generation of charismatic leaders. Character development in our youth is of vital importance to us all. So join the adventures today!
