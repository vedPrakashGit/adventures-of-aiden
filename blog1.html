New Children’s Book
Series Stands Up
To Bullying

A newly-released children’s book series titled The Adventures of Aiden addresses multiple issues facing children of every age. One of these most critical issues is bullying.

In The Adventures of Aiden, readers follow the struggles and feats of Aiden and Skylar, two siblings bound for a lifetime of learning and excitement, as well as their friends.

Many educators, authors, and activists take a gander at the fight against bullying in adolescents - and many succeed. However, the author of The Adventures Of Aiden takes that fight a step further. He had a different approach in mind to fight taunting, teasing, and harassment amongst young children.

AOA series’ author wrote each word of the tales with a blaring motto in mind: begin within.

Where so many educators, authors, and activists focus on fighting the bully, The Adventures Of Aiden shows children how to prevent bullying from wavering their sense of self. For, if a knight has built a strong armor, no sword can penetrate deep enough to injure them!

The author of the Adventures of Aiden series believed that the only true way to combat bullying was not to tackle the bully, but to combat the way one handles the bully’s actions. People can’t control what others do; they can only control how they handle it. With this key lesson, children can move forward in their lives deciding how others can make them feel, not the other way around.

Though geared specifically at the age ranges of Kindergarten through fifth grade, the AOA Series’ lessons about bullying apply to every age group.

In one statement, AOA’s author draws parallels between getting a flu vaccine and equipping children with the tools to prevent - and beat - bullying.

He explains, “Many parents make sure their child gets a flu vaccine each year, providing a much-needed layer of protection against nausea and runny noses. As different as bullies and the flu may seem, there’s a lesson to be learned from getting your yearly shot. Parents protect their children from the flu by changing their children from within, altering their defense mechanisms to be able to fight the sickness from the inside out.

No parent would ever consider trying to change the behavior of the flu to protect their child; that would be impossible. Instead, they begin within their child, changing what they can control instead of wishing for the impossible.”

The Adventures of Aiden series is a child’s bully vaccine. Instead of hoping to change the bully, the Adventures of Aiden series encourages children to begin within and build healthy self-esteem to help them ward off the effects of taunting. The book series also offers resources for parents, educators, and other guardians on their website.

One can’t control what bullies do to their child when a parent is not around. But parents can provide their child with the shot of onfidence necessary to develop an immunity to bullying.

And with The Adventures of Aiden series, doing so just got easier!
