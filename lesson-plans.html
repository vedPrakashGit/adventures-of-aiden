Bullying ends when we begin within.

Join Us

The Adventures of Aiden series encourages children to fend off bullying from the inside out. These cheering and poignant stories follow main character Aiden as he learns how to ignore teasing and helps his friends do the same. Aiden and his friends set a great example on positive, constructive ways to respond to and ignore bullying, making it easier for your child to go to school with a smile on his or her face each day.

Title:

The Mirror Within

Objectives:
  • NCES.RED.SE.1.2 - Identify ways of controlling behaviors associated with emotional states, feelings, and moods.
  • NCES.RED.SE.2 - Understand the relationship between self and others in the broader world.
  • NCES.RED.SE.1 - Understand the meaning and importance of personal responsibility and self-awareness.
Essential Question:

Why is it important how we see ourselves and how can we maintain a positive view?

Teacher Input:

Today we are going to read the book The Mirror Within, use that book to discuss how important it is that we maintain a positive outlook about ourselves and what truly makes us beautiful, and then do an activity to show how beautiful we are on the inside.

Guided Practice:
  • Teacher gives an intro and then reads aloud the book.
  • Teacher leads discussion (based on grade level) with questions prompts.
  • Teacher will give instructions to the “Creating a Positive Mirror” activity and then we will complete it with our classmates. /li>
Independent Practice:

Students will work around the room and help create “positive mirrors” for their classmates

Informal Assessment (Exit Ticket):

(To be completed as a written exit ticket) Which three positive attributes do you think make you the most beautiful on the inside? Explain why.
